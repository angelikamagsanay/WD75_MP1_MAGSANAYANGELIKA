Pampanga's List of Festivals


Dukit Festival

Part of the celebration of the feast of St. James the Great, patron saint of Betis, Guagua, Pampanga

Where: Guagua, Pampanga
When: December 27, 2023-December 28, 2023

Duman Festival

The sprit of "food tripping". It is considered among the most important holiday events of Kapampangans in December and they try not to miss it.

Where: Sta. Rita Pampanga
When: first Saturday of December 2023

Frog Festival

This festival shows the traditional way of catching frogs with a bamboo stick and a line with earth called "paduasan"

Where: San Fernando City
When: October 12, 2023-October 15, 2023

Philippine International Hot Air Balloon Fiesta

You can watch colorful hot air balloons take off and fly over the Clark Freeport Zone. There are also special-shaped balloons, such as animals, cartoon characters, and other designs.

Where: Clark, Pampanga
When: April 12, 2023- April 13,2023

Giant Lantern Festival (Ligligan Parul)

The Giant Lantern Festival celebrates Christmas by making huge lanterns designed with different lights and colors.

Where: San Fernando, Pampanga
When: December 18, 2023 to January 1, 2024

Makatapak Festival

The famous re-enactment of the townfolks who walked barefoot through the lahar caused by the eruption of Mt. Pinatubo.

Where: Apalit, Pampanga
When: November 19, 2023

San Pedro Cutud Lenten Rites

Holy Week re-enactment of Christ's Passion and Death.

Where: San Pedro Cutud, Pampanga
When: April 7th 2023

Sinukwan Festival

A week-long festival mainly focused on giving praise to the Kapampangan god Aring Sinukwan, who is believed to be a deity since ancient times.
Where: San Fernando, Pampanga
When:December 1, 2023
