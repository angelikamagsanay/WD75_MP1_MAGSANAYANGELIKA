How do I get there?

By Air


You can also take a bus or drive to the Clark Freeport Zone. If you are coming from Manila, take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and exit at Dau, Mabalacat. From there, take the Clark South Exit and follow the signs to the festival venue.


By Land


The Philippine National Railways (PNR) also has a station in Clark, which is about 2 kilometers away from the festival venue. You can take a tricycle or a shuttle service from the station to the festival.


By Train


The Philippine National Railways (PNR) also has a station in Clark, which is about 2 kilometers away from the festival venue. You can take a tricycle or a shuttle service from the station to the festival.
